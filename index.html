A Simple Bookmark Manager

A clean and simple interface to organise your favourite websites. Open a new browser tab and see your sites load instantly. Try it for free.

Get it on Chrome Get it on Firefox Get it on Opera

Features

Our aim is to make it quick and easy for you to access your favourite websites. Your bookmarks sync between your devices so you can access them on the go.

Bookmarks in once click

Organise your bookmarks however you like. Our simple drag-and-drop interface gives you complete control over how you manage your favourite sites.
